Project Overview

Who knew that making friends was a good thing?
Enjoy browsing through Cohort Love Letter, a website that displays information about the members of the Flatiron School 08/30/21 Software Engineering cohort.

Prior to creating this website, I sent out a short survey that most of the cohort members filled out and sent back to me. I then used that information to fill out the data in the backend.

I definitely wouldn't have made it through this program without all the people in our cohort. Everyone was kind and patient and supported each other and really brought out the best in me. Thank you always! ðŸ’˜
